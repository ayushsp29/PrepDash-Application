How It Works: A User Manual

Get started with Prep Dash in a few simple steps.

STEP 1

Customize Your Schedule & Habits

Navigate to the **Settings** view (the gear icon). Here, you can define your recurring daily schedule by adding tasks with specific times. Below the timetable, you can add the habits you want to track, along with their start and end dates. Click "Save" after making changes.

STEP 2

Track Your Daily Progress

In the **Today** view, you'll see all your scheduled tasks and active habits for the current day. Simply check the box next to each item as you complete it. Your daily progress bar will update in real-time.

STEP 3

Analyze Your Performance

Go to the **Progress** view (the chart icon) to see your data visualized. You can review your weekly completion rate, see a detailed breakdown of your progress in each subject, and watch your habit consistency curve grow with each successful day.
