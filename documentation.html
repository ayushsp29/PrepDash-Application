Current Limitations

  • No User Accounts: The app currently uses anonymous authentication. There is no sign-up/login system, so data is tied to a single browser session.
  • Single User Focus: The application is designed for individual use and does not support collaboration or team features.
  • No Offline Support: An active internet connection is required to sync data with Firebase.

Future Scope

  • Full Authentication: Implementing email/password and social logins to allow users to access their data across multiple devices.
  • Data Export: Adding functionality to export progress reports and schedules as PDF or CSV files.
  • Mobile App: Developing native iOS and Android applications for a better mobile experience.
  • Gamification: Introducing points, badges, and leaderboards to further motivate users.

How It Works: A User Manual

Get started with Prep Dash in a few simple steps.

STEP 1

Customize Your Schedule & Habits

Navigate to the **Settings** view (the gear icon). Here, you can define your recurring daily schedule by adding tasks with specific times. Below the timetable, you can add the habits you want to track, along with their start and end dates. Click "Save" after making changes.

STEP 2

Track Your Daily Progress

In the **Today** view, you'll see all your scheduled tasks and active habits for the current day. Simply check the box next to each item as you complete it. Your daily progress bar will update in real-time.

STEP 3

Analyze Your Performance

Go to the **Progress** view (the chart icon) to see your data visualized. You can review your weekly completion rate, see a detailed breakdown of your progress in each subject, and watch your habit consistency curve grow with each successful day.

STEP 4

Get a High-Level Overview

Click on the **Full Schedule** view (the grid icon) to see a complete spreadsheet of all your planned activities. This view is perfect for long-term planning and identifying busy periods in advance. Completed tasks are highlighted in green so you can see your progress at a glance.
